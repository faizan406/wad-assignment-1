Admission

Admission in the department for various degree programmes are offered in Spring and Fall semesters.

PhD (Computer Science) – Every Semester

PhD is highest degree awarded in academic discipline and is the most satisfying and rewarding educational experience. In our PhD programme, students work closely with a faculty member, performing original research, and tackling challenging and unsolved problems.

Admission Requirements

  • Degree from HEC recognized university as per university rules.
  • The minimum CGPA for admission in PhD is 3.0/4.0 in MS/MPhil degree or 60% marks in annual system in the relevant field like Computer Science, Software Engineering, Information Technology, or related disciplines.
  • Must pass the test and interview arranged by the department.

MPhil (Computer Science) – Fall Semester

The objective of M.Phil Computer Science programme is to train students to initiate research work.

Admission Requirements

  • 4 years BS (Computer Science, Software Engineering, Information Technology or related disciplines) or 2 years M.Sc. (Computer Science, Software Engineering, Information Technology).
  • Must pass test (Sample test available) and interview arranged by the department.
Click here to see more details about the programme

MS (Information Science & Technology) – Every Semester

The objective of MS program in Information Science & Technology (IST) is to design and develop information related applications using information technology by understanding relationship between digital information, people and technology. The class schedule is flexible for professionals working in the industry. The classes are held in the evening and usually two courses are offered to the students, so that they can enhance their knowledge and skills along with their jobs.

Admission Requirements

  • 16 years of education, 4 years BS or M.Sc in Computing related disciplines with experience in digital information management.
  • Must pass test and interview arranged by the department.
Click here to see more details about the programme

MS (Data Science) – Every Semester

The students will be exposed to different aspects of data science including programming, data structures, and algorithms for data, data analysis and visualization, and big data analytics. These aspects will provide the students the opportunities to effectively work in local and international markets as well as to pursue a PhD degree in data science.

Admission Requirements

  • BS or MS (16 years of education) in computing related disciplines OR
  • BS or MS (16 years of education) in non-computing disciplines having studied at least two mathematics courses, two computing courses OR one mathematics and one computing course OR "Data Structures", and "Data Analytics". These courses may be exempted, if the applicant has already studied them in his or her terminal degree.
  • Must pass test and interview arranged by the department.

BS (Computer Science) – Fall Semester

The program enables students to design, implement and manage systems combining scientific, computational, communication and multimedia facilities in different domains.

Admission Requirements

  • Intermediate with Mathematics
  • Intermediate without Mathematics (with deficiency courses) OR
  • Equivalent Certificate of 12 years education with Mathematics
