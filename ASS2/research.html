Research

The department has a strong research focus with faculty and students actively participating in research.

Software Engineering Research Group

Software engineering research group is actively involved in research related to software architecture, reverse engineering, program comprehension, and software maintenance.

Faculty

  • Dr. Onaiza Maqbool

Research Interests

  • Software reengineering
  • Software architecture recovery
  • Program understanding
  • Machine learning

Database and Information Systems Research Group

This group focuses on database systems, information systems, distributed systems, and data warehousing/mining.

Faculty

  • Dr. Khalid Saleem

Research Interests

  • Database Systems, Information Systems
  • Distributed Systems
  • Data Warehousing/Mining

Human-Centered Computing Research Group

This group works on usability and accessibility of interactive systems, web engineering, and applied aspects of ontologies.

Faculty

  • Dr. Shuaib Karim

Research Interests

  • Usability/Accessibility of Interactive Systems
  • Web Engineering
  • Applied aspects of ontologies

Social Computing and Data Science Research Group

This group explores social computing, network science, and data science.

Faculty

  • Dr. Rabeeh Ayaz Abbasi

Research Interests

  • Social Computing
  • Network Science
  • Data Science

Pattern Recognition and Computational Intelligence Research Group

This group focuses on pattern recognition, image processing, and computational intelligence.

Faculty

  • Dr. Ayaz Hussain

Research Interests

  • Pattern Recognition
  • Image Processing
  • Computational Intelligence

Networks and Security Research Group

This group works on IoT, intelligent networks, blockchain-based security, vehicular networks, and multimedia networks.

Faculty

  • Dr. Muazzam A Khan Khattak

Research Interests

  • Internet of Things, Next Generation Intelligent Networks
  • Block Chain Based Information and Network Security
  • Vehicular Ad-hoc and Sensor Actor Networks
  • Acoustic and Multimedia Networks
